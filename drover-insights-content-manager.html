QUALITY
/03 · Capabilities

What we improve

A01
Lighting & Color Balance

Correct exposure, white balance, and color cast issues from mixed lighting or harsh conditions.

A02
Perspective Correction

Straighten verticals, correct lens distortion, and fix converging lines from wide-angle phone cameras.

A03
Crop & Composition

Reframe for impact. Remove distracting edges, center the subject, improve visual hierarchy.

A04
Glare Reduction

Reduce reflections on windows, screens, polished surfaces, and wet materials where possible.

A05
Appropriate Cleanup

Remove temporary clutter, equipment shadows, and minor distractions. Never structural elements or permanent features.

A06
Sharpening & Detail

Recover detail in soft or slightly blurred images. Enhance texture without introducing artifacts.

⚠ All enhancements are realistic. We do not fabricate features, alter structural elements, or produce results that misrepresent the actual subject.
